Who We Are

The Stuffies at ASU® exists to spread laughter and fun across Arizona State University and beyond through Stuffies of all kinds. College life is stressful; we get it! And we believe that a simple stuffed animal or plushy has the power to lighten a mood, draw a smile, and bring cheer to any atmosphere. That's why we are committed to taking our Stuffies with us everywhere - classrooms, dining courts, events, you name it - to bring a smile to YOUR face and the faces of everyone we meet.

Not only that, but we desire to bring the joy of Stuffies into our surrounding communities through Stuffies donations. Salute our nation's heroes and their families on Veteran's Day, play the role of Santa for patients in hospitals during the Christmas season, and so much more! One stuffed animal can make a huge difference in someone's life, and we exist to make that difference happen.
